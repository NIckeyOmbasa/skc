About Us

Learn about our mission, history, and leadership

Our Mission

The South Kenya Conference of Seventh-day Adventists is committed to proclaiming the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ to all people, leading them to accept Jesus as their personal Savior and to unite with His church, nurturing them in preparation for His soon return.
For more about our beliefs visit our beliefs page to learn more.

Our History

South Kenya Conference was organized in 1912 and holds the history as being the mother conference in Kenya. It has undergone reorganizations in 1953, 1981, 2018. It serves members from most parts of Kisii County. View Departments

Over the years, the conference has grown significantly, establishing numerous churches, schools, and healthcare facilities throughout the region. Today, it continues to serve as a vital center for Seventh-day Adventist ministry in South Kenya.

Our Institutions

Nyanchwa Adventist College

A premier institution of higher learning offering quality education in various disciplines. The college is committed to academic excellence and spiritual development, preparing students for both professional success and Christian service.

Nyanchwa Adventist Secondary School

A leading secondary school known for its strong academic performance and holistic education. The school nurtures young minds through quality education, spiritual guidance, and character development.
P.O. Box 1020; 40200 Kisii; Kenya.

Nyanchwa Adventist Preparatory School

Providing foundational education with a focus on academic excellence and Christian values. The school creates a nurturing environment where young learners can develop their potential and grow in faith.

Nyanchwa Adventist Mission Hospital

A comprehensive healthcare facility offering quality medical services to the community. The hospital combines modern medical care with compassionate service, reflecting Christ's healing ministry.
P.O. Box 22; Kisii; Kenya.

Gesusu Dispensary

A healthcare facility offering medical services to the community bringing health services closer to the people. The hospital combines modern medical care with compassionate service, reflecting Christ's healing ministry.
P.O. Box 3; Gesusu, Kisii; Kenya

Beyond these flagship institutions, the South Kenya Conference sponsors and supports over 50 church-sponsored institutions across the conference, including schools, health facilities, and community development centers, all working together to serve our communities and spread God's love.
